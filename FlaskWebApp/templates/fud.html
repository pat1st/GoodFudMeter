Welcome to Tesla News Fud-Parade

This service is for Tesla analysts and other people who are interested in Tesla and how it is being / made to be perceived in public at the very moment. For this we make use of AI (Artificial Intelligence) to determine the actual MSM (Main-Stream-Media) FUD (Fear, Uncertaincy, Doubt) level for TESLA.

Tesla does not have a PR department and does not advertise, but still is very successful, due to the great products and their strategy to implement their mission, to "accelerate the world's transition to sustainable energy". It is widely known that a lot of FUD is spread via MSM to undermine Tesla and also manipulate the $TSLA Stock price.

We look up the top 'Tesla' News articles in Google News and use Google AI to see if the article's title is sentimented as good or bad. Then we sum it up and list it for your reference.

Today we offer this service in 5 languages: English, German, French, Spanish and Chinese, with Chinese being in test mode for now. Please get in touch (via Twitter, see below) if you want languages added or have other ideas, how this could be improved..
